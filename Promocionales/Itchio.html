Terracota



Local signal is a narrative game with multiple choices and many endings. Help the emergency line User escape from the danger in which they woke up. Or guide them to discover the truth about why they lost their memory.



  • Notes:
  • Each game lasts between 5 to 10 minutes.
  • Short story with many secrets to discover.
  • Multiple and unlockable routes, many endings.
  • If you want to discover the truth, the total time is approximately 3 hours.
