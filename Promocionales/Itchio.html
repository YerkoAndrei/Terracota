Defeat your opponents with your fortresses and cannon!


Terracota is a 1v1 turn-based physics game, where you create your own fortress and test them in combat with your cannon skills.

You can play on local or LAN.


Play as the Host or Guest battalion


Defeat the enemy terracotta monkey warriors


Notes:

  • You should be able to play through the internet with a virtual LAN.
  • This project was made to test my skills on networking with C# and the Stride engine.

Future plans:

  • Bepu physics implementation in Stride.
  • Linux support.
  • Android support.

If someday, for some reason, this project is activated, there will be open matchmaking, with dedicated servers and ingame chat!

Any feedback or bugs report is very appreciated.
